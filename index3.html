The Story

Clair ventures into the first strange and enigmatic area, where he finds a locked portal with a message beside it: "You cannot go back, but you can move forward." In the depths of the luminous forest, he encounters his first enemies: glowing alien beings coursing with electric charges. He discovers that these creatures are guarding something valuable.
